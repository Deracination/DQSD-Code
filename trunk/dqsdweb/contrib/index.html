[ Dave's Quick Search Deskbar ]

This page is for DQSD searches that are only applicable to a small percentage of DQSD users, such as localized searches -- although some would definitely say I'm being a bigot because of all the US searches that are part of the install package. Because of that there is definitely a fuzzy line between when we should distribute and what is provided on this page. Also, this page may simply provide a hub for pointing folks to other sites with DQSD contributions.

Searches

Miscellanous
  • Tom Corcoran - http://www.reducekeystrokes.com/dqsd
    Lots of useful DQSD information, some UK-specific bits and pieces.

  • Shawn K. Hall - http://ReliableAnswers.com/x/dqsd/
    Shawn created several utility type searches (including a VBScript parser and Terminal Services director). Many more at his site.

  • Ryan Carpenter - http://home.comcast.net/~ryan_carpenter/dqsd/
    Ryan's stuff includes a great bugmenot search.

Swedish
  • Kim Gräsman - http://www.winwonk.com/swe/searches/
    I made my searches available in case anybody's interested. I doubt anybody outside of Sweden will find them useful, so the download page is all Swedish.

Add-ons

Brent Beardsley's Add-ons
  • Bookmarks
    Display your bookmarks from Internet Explorer, Mozilla, Netscape 4, or Yahoo Companion in the DQSD popup menu.

  • Desktop
    Display your desktop icons as menu items in the DQSD popup menu.

  • Quick Launch
    Display your quick launch icons as menu items in the DQSD popup menu.

Steve Babineau's Add-ons
  • iptools
    ipconfig-like functionality as a DQSD add-on.

Miscellaneous
